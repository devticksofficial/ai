Pick a rhyme. Watch similar top viewed rhymes. Compe up with an overall story and give prompt to chatGPT. 

Current Workflow: 
1. generate Music ( AICovergen ( Music + vocals) : https://github.com/flaglow/AICoverGen.git)
2. Generate video script: ChatGPT
3. Generate image and video generation prompts to feed to AI model: ChatGPT
4. Generate images: Playground.ai: https://huggingface.co/playgroundai
5. Generate videos: DynamiCrafter: https://github.com/Doubiiu/DynamiCrafter 
6. Put the generated video clips in a video: ClipChamp (https://app.clipchamp.com/)
